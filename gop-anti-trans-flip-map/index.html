The GOP's Anti-Trans Push

2023 has seen an explosion in Republican-led anti-trans legislation.

Republicans fully control the government in 22 states. More than 2/3 of those states have already passed anti-trans legislation that would prevent transgender people from receiving access to critical medical care, ban LGBTQ+ friendly books, and create a surveillance state around bodily autonomy.

Democrats Fighting Back

While more than 2/3 of Republican states have passed legislation targeting trans Americans, Democrats have fought back.

All but one of the democratic-control states have laws in place banning discrimination based on gender identity and sexuality in housing, jobs and public spaces.

But there is still much more to do!
